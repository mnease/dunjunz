# Dunjunz worldbuilding level-up — research v1

**Status:** Research only. **No code this round.**  
**Date:** 2026-07-22  
**Council:** Scout (canon influences + gaps), Forager (design systems / room–encounter grammar), Waggle (contrarian traps + topologies)  
**External anchors:** Zelda 1 / ALttP dungeon craft, OSR (Keep on the Borderlands, Barrowmaze, Veins of the Earth, Mörk Borg / Shadowdark), roguelikes & crawlers (Isaac, Gungeon, Spelunky, Hades, Dead Cells, Darkest Dungeon, Shiren / Mystery Dungeon, Grimrock / Etrian), LitRPG (*Dungeon Crawler Carl* floor seasons / factions / rule injections)  
**Constraint (locked):** Game **modes** (Hard, Army, etc.) and their save contracts do **not** change. World, dungeons, creeps, land topology, and ceremony placement **may** change next round.

**North star (council merge):**  
Dunjunz should become a funny wound in a **living map**: each land a hungry pressure with its own laws of motion—not a copy-paste basement with a new tint. Creeps are factions in a food web; hazards are their tools; bosses are **different kinds of ceremony**; mid tension is territory and room rules, not another employee handbook. Humor is sharp, local, and educational; dread is the quiet between jokes. Greatness is bought with **asymmetric topology, ecological roles, and set-pieces**—not more rooms, more identical floors, or more middle managers.

### 1D. Dunjunz baseline (today)

- **Hub:** Meadow → trail fork (Woodz N / Dezertz S) / Dunjunz stairs.  
- **Dunjunz:** Long vertical spine (~B1–B8), deep floors largely **foyer–hall–side–descent** stamps; gel cube dual-path den; mid-wardens (Captain B4, Lawyer B6).  
- **Woodz / Dezertz:** Short **surface** land-boss paths; deep wings post-clear optional (Howl / Lease dens).  
- **Sewerz:** Kingdomz job path; shorter pipe spine; Assistant Honk + Goose.  
- **Strengths already:** Quest sitcom spine, intentional misspellings, soft mid doctrine (no portal/land clear), cube dual outcome, set-pieces (redshirts, Prizella “rented monthly”).  
- **Weaknesses:** Cookie-cutter deep topology; land identity thinner in deep; creeps as species lists not factions; side rooms often loot closets; replay mostly modes/systems not graph.

## 2. Load-bearing world principles (council consensus)

### Topology

1. **Graph before skin** — Loops, cul-de-sacs, multi-stair meaning, asymmetric wings. Skinning one template × N floors is depth inflation.  
2. **Verticality needs a new rule per band** — Not only tint + HP (hazard type, who owns the floor, navigation grammar).  
3. **Landmarks beat length** — Players remember cube / badge check / throne, not the 17th hall.  
4. **Overworld is political** — Trail as scar/hub; approaches imply economy and danger (Borderlands), not only N/S labels.  
5. **Asymmetric land length** — Not every land needs an 8-floor spine (Zelda, DCSS branches). Celebrate short spicy lands + one long hell + endgame.

### Ecology & creeps

6. **Factions + niches**, not deeper-enemy lists — Who eats whom, who patrols vs nests, invasive vs native.  
7. **Joke creeps need jobs** — Redshirts/managers work because they imply bureaucracy of the underworld.  
8. **Roles over headcount** — Chaff / line / skirmisher / hazard body / tank / elite / mid-warden / land boss.  
9. **Non-combat verbs** — Talk, bribe, avoid, pit factions (cube / Lawyer already gold).  
10. **Keystone death is world news** — Land clear should change spawn mix or signs, not only a flag toast.

### Pacing & dens

11. **Peaks every ~3 floors or ~8–10 critical rooms**, rotating peak *types* (elite, hazard set-piece, social, mid, rest, true boss).  
12. **No double-ceremony** — Mid immediately before land boss flattens both (already rejected for Woodz/Dezertz surface).  
13. **Quiet rooms are content** — Foyers as thresholds (R0), not more packs.  
14. **Optional dens = unique room rules**, not only unique HP (cube peaceful-until-hit; Lawyer talk).  
15. **Critical path = story rope; dens = world** — Rope completable alone; dens for mastery/lore/brags.

### Soft gates & ceremony

16. **Soft gates > hard locks** for Zelda-like humor (run past, knowledge, talk, bribe). Hard gates only for major story limbs.  
17. **Keys open graphs**, not only the next door.  
18. **Strict ceremony hierarchy** — Land boss owns portal / land clear / boss chest / monologue length. Mids never promote to second DM.  
19. **One true rite per land; many clerks** — Mid-management comedy depends on a singular apex (DM / Goose / Lord / Wraith).

### Humor & dread

20. **Joke first, then the punch lands hard** — Funny name, lethal verb.  
21. **Comedy of systems > comedy of only names.**  
22. **Dread is spatial** — Silence, return cost, ecology, hazards as faction tools.  
23. **One joke layer per room max** (name *or* prop *or* dialog).  
24. **Office comedy is Dunjunz-local** — Cap the org-chart gag; other lands use pack / lease / bilge / folk.  
25. **Earnest goals never become bits** — Saving Prizella is sacred; HR of the underworld is the bit.

### Replay without modes

26. **Knowledge, order, dual dens outcomes, secret wings, world-state lines** — Not mode forks.  
27. **Authored landmarks + light seasoning** — Not full roguelike wipe of Prizella continuity.  
28. **Carl-lite “ordinances”** — After land clear, signs/rules reframe old rooms (hand-authored flags).  
29. **Misspellings stay canon texture** — Never “correct” woodz/mapz/forjing.

## 3. Room grammar (Forager design system)

Rooms are **verbs**, not scenery skins.

| Room type | Promise | Density | Dunjunz today |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Threshold / Foyer | Orient, breathe, commit | Low | Often over-combat |
| Spine / Hall | Progress + pressure | Med–High | Default combat |
| Den / Side | Risk–reward optional | Med | Often chest + 2 creeps |
| Arena | Fair named duel | Focused | Boss / mid sides |
| Vault | Payday | Low hostiles | Boss loot, mapz |
| Shrine / Rest | Recover | Zero hostiles | Old man, tinkerer |
| Rule / Puzzle | Non-combat skill | Variable | Lawyer / cube pattern |
| Hazard artery | Space as enemy | Hazards > creeps | Deep lava/water |
| Hub node | Choice | Low combat | Meadow, trail, edges |
| Ceremony | Land clear | Solo (+ adds) | Land bosses only |
| Secret / loop | Discovery | Low–med | **Underbuilt** |

**Jaquays minimum (next round targets):**

- ≥2 paths between important rooms (loop or branch) on long lands.  
- ≥1 secret that shortcuts or vaults—not required for land clear.  
- Secrets connect to another room/lore scrap.

### Side-room 5-cycle (replace homogeneous combat sides)

Example Dunjunz: **Vault → Nest → Mid-warden → Rule/Ambush → Quiet/Hazard vault** (rotate; don’t invent a new verb every floor).

### Encounter recipes (role mix)

| Code | Composition | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| R0 Quiet | 0 hostiles + sign | Foyers |
| R1 Pair | 2 line | Early hall |
| R2 Triangle | 2 chaff + 1 line | Low grind |
| R3 Pinch | Skirmisher + hazard body | Approaches |
| R4 Nest | 3–4 chaff + tank | Optional dens |
| R5 Escort | Elite + 2 chaff | Deep hall |
| R6 Soft mid | Mid-warden alone | Arena side |
| R7 Hazard stew | 1–2 hostiles + heavy tiles | Maze bands |
| R8 Ambush | Empty enter → provoke on chest/talk | Cube-class |
| R9 Ceremony | Land boss | Boss room |
| R10 Overpack | “Too many” — flee OK | Optional only |

**Cadence:** Peak about every 3 floors / ~8–10 critical rooms (Hexis + mid-bosses v1). Cap consecutive pure chase rooms at 2.

## 4. Creep ecology redesign (not more sprites first)

### Factions (≤3 per major land)

| Land | Factions | Non-fight verb seeds |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Dunjunz** | Clipboard Order (staff/mids/DM); Redshirt union; Deep fauna (invasive wolves) | Talk, bribe, badge ignore, steal clipboard |
| **Woodz** | Pack law; Hollow spirits / trespass forje folk | Howl truce, avoid dens, feed/spare Howl |
| **Dezertz** | Leasehold undead; Dune pests; Tower claim | Lease notice, heat path, deposit gag |
| **Sewerz** | Royal plumbers; Bilge mutants; Goose court | Pipe permit, waddle past, tax scroll bit |
| **Surface** | Prey nursery / contested edge | Quest, not combat farm |

### Role taxonomy (map species into jobs)

Chaff · Line · Skirmisher · Hazard body · Tank · Elite · Support/stress · Mid-warden · Land boss · (optional) Wanderer.

**Hard rule:** Woodz/Dezertz deep halls must not default to slime+skeleton unless that *is* the ecology. Land-lock pack recipes.

### Keystone bosses = different ceremony *kinds*

| Land | Ceremony type | Ecology after kill (fiction) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dunjunz | Meta-authority / throne | Bureaucracy fractures; signs update |
| Woodz | Territory / apex | Pack too quiet or too bold |
| Dezertz | Hostage / landlord | Heat “eases”; politics heat up |
| Sewerz | Absurd sovereignty | Bilge + tax aftermath |

Do **not** “fix” early surface bosses by forcing every land into B8-only climax.

### Mid-management gag — declare victory

- **Freeze** org-chart wardens as **Dunjunz-local** (Captain, Lawyer already shipped).  
- Other lands: ecology elites and dens rules, **not** more HR titles.  
- Deep dens: pack / lease / bilge identity (Howl, Lease, Honk already point the right way).

## 8. Humor / dread acceptance tests (Waggle)

1. One joke must teach a rule (or cut it).  
2. Dread gets silence after monologue.  
3. Office comedy stays Dunjunz-local.  
4. Boss joke peaks once per land (Wolf Lord’s “never met her” is the model—don’t clone refuse-plot).  
5. Never undercut a death mid-swing.  
6. Hard Mode may be less funny (meaner mix, same world).  
7. Earnest quest lines never become bits.

## 9. What NOT to steal (kills Dunjunz voice)

- Grimdark misery tourism without punchlines.  
- Soulsborne mute environmental only.  
- Full roguelike wipe of authored continuity.  
- ARPG loot ocean as “world.”  
- Boss every N floors.  
- Hard stair locks on mids.  
- More lands with same 8-floor spine.  
- Joke names without ecology.  
- Tomb of Horrors on critical path.  
- LitRPG spreadsheet dialog.  
- Mode-like world forks (easy path vs army geography).  
- Second Dunjun Master per land.  
- “Prestige” rebrand that erases mapz/woodz misspellings.

## 11. Concrete redesign levers (cheat sheet)

1. Break 4-room stamp on landmark floors (B1, B4, B6, B8, 1–2 wildcards).  
2. One shortcut/column per long land (knowledge, not mode).  
3. Woodz/Dezertz deep = true second story (pack war / lease hell), not residual halls.  
4. ~40–60% of sides become rule rooms (shrine, faction border, trap gallery, peaceful NPC).  
5. Three factions per land + territory signs.  
6. Dual-outcome dens pattern land-wide (spare/kill changes later sign or soft shortcut).  
7. Peak card deck per land (~6–8 peaks on long spine).  
8. Foreshadow 2 floors ahead on signs.  
9. One unique navigation fantasy per land (procedure / flanks / exposure / pipes).  
10. Story mats from dens (pack fang, lease stamp, bilge pearl)—teach land.  
11. Kingdomz comments dens you skipped after land clear.  
12. Strengthen approaches (antechamber evidence) more than boss HP.  
13. Department fiction: managed floors *look* managed around Captain; errata footnotes around Lawyer.  
14. Branch-order gossip (woodz-before-dezertz).  
15. Secret mapz cells as replay bait.  
16. Hand-authored “ordinances” after clears (Carl-lite).  
17. Optional hard dens (Tomb-pocket) for brag only—never story gate.

## 12. Success criteria for “greatest dungeon crawler” world pass

A player can explain each land in **one sentence** by its **laws**, not its tint.  
A friend hears a set-piece story (cube, badge, lease, goose) without being told HP numbers.  
Skipping every den still completes Prizella’s arc; clearing dens feels like mastering a living place.  
Hard and Army still work unchanged.  
No one needs a wiki for the critical path; dens reward cartographers and weirdos.

**Failed pass signals:** longer wiki, taller basement, more clipboard bosses, same foyer-hall-side-descent forever, Woodz deep still “B3 Dunjunz green.”
